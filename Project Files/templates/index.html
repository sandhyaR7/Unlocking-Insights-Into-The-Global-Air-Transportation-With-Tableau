Introduction

Data Analytics with Tableau

  • The air transport system generally includes airports, Air traffic control(ATC) system,routes and airlines
  • Air transport can transport goods to areas that are not easily accessible by other means of transport. Suitable for transporting high-value or perishable goods over long distances.
  • A global system of flight operations, connecting thousands of different destinations through both direct services and services via major hub airports.

The Global Air Transportation Network dataset is a comprehensive collection of information on airports, airlines and their routes. It contains information such as names, cities, countries, codes (IATA and ICAO) longitudes, latitudes and altitudes of airports across the world with detailed time zone and daylight saving time data. Additionally, this includes information about airlines including their IDs, name aliases, IATA and ICAO codes, callsigns country of origin and active/inactive status.
